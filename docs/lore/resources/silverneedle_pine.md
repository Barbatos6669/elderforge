# Silverneedle Pine

Silverneedle Pine is one of the first sacred trees players encounter around
Hearthgrove. It grows tall and straight, with pale bark and long blue-green
needles that shine silver under moonlight.

Old woodcutters say the tree was created when Lunara, the Silver Veil, spilled
moonlight onto the forests of Veyra, the Green Mother. Since then,
Silverneedle Pine has been seen as a bridge between nature magic and moon
magic.

## In-Game Description

**Silverneedle Pine**

A tall, pale-barked pine whose needles glow faintly beneath moonlight. Its wood
is soft, straight, and easy to shape, making it prized by carpenters, bowyers,
and rune-carvers.

Villagers say a Silverneedle remembers every oath spoken beneath its branches.

## Resource Names

- Tree node: Silverneedle Pine
- Raw resource: Silverneedle Logs
- Refined resource: Silverneedle Beams
- Rare drop: Silverpine Resin
- Magical rare drop: Moonlit Resin
- Crafting extra: Silverneedle Cones

## Crafting Identity

Silverneedle Pine is the starter magical wood. It is common enough for new
players to gather, but special enough to teach that even basic resources have
lore, spirits, and crafting value.

It is especially useful for beginner bows, wooden shields, staves, house beams,
shop signs, crafting tables, shrine repairs, ward posts around town, and
low-tier magic charms. The wood is light, soft, straight-grained, and holds
simple enchantments well.

## Harvest Belief

Woodcutters have a rule:

> Take the trunk, leave the cone.

When cutting a Silverneedle Pine, gatherers traditionally leave one pinecone
buried near the stump. This is believed to calm the forest spirit and help
another tree grow. Players who harvest corrupted Silverneedle trees may anger
nearby forest spirits.

## Magical Variant

At night, some trees become **Moon-Touched Silverneedle Pine**.

These trees glow softly with silver-blue light and can drop Moonlit Resin for
mana charms, moon staves, glowing lanterns, anti-curse wards, dream magic
items, and stealth or illusion gear.

## Enemy Hooks

**Needlekin** are tiny forest spirits made of pine needles, bark, and
moonlight. They are not evil, but they attack people who cut too much or
harvest corrupted groves.

**Silverbark Wardens** are large tree spirits that protect ancient
Silverneedle groves.

## Starting Quest Hook

**The Trees Remember**

A carpenter in Hearthgrove needs Silverneedle Beams to repair the town gate,
but the nearby grove has become hostile.

Quest beats:

- Gather Silverneedle Logs.
- Defeat corrupted Needlekin.
- Find a broken shrine stone in the grove.
- Leave a pinecone offering.
- Return to town and craft Silverneedle Beams.

Rewards:

- Woodcutting XP.
- Basic bow recipe.
- Silverneedle Beam recipe.
- Small reputation gain with the gatherers.

NPC rumor:

> Never cut a Silverneedle under a blood moon. The wood screams, and the forest
> remembers your name.
